About
Insightic is a spin-off company from the National University of Singapore (NUS), which has recently been recognized as the 8th best globally and the 1st in Asia as per the Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) World University Rankings 2024.

Founded by Dr. Lu Shengliang and Professor He Bingsheng, the foundation of Insightic lies in their extensive research spanning decades in Graph Analytics and Federated Learning. Their expertise is further solidified with the publication of over 100 related academic papers. Insightic's core mission is to harness AI capabilities to provide compliance solutions tailored to the digital asset industry.
